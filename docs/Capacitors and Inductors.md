Capacitors in parallel summate as
$$C_{parallel}=\sum_{i=1}^nC_{i}$$
and in series,
$$C_{series}=\sum_{i=1}^n \frac{1}{C_{i}}$$
An inductor opposes change in current by storing energy in its magnetic field
$$V_{L}= L \frac{dI}{dt}$$
